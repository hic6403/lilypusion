All About GHS Best Buddies

What is Best Buddies?

Best Buddies is an organization that works in countries worldwide to provide people who have IDD (intellectual and developmental disabilities) with friendships and bring awareness to the fact that people with IDD are still people.

In Grapevine, Best Buddies has helped to forge relationships between average students and students with IDD. The RISE program offers a place for many of these students to work together at their pace. But, some best buddies are in regular classes. Best Buddies has impacted their lives in a positive way and helped them make bonds that last forever.

Who are the Best Buddies?

Each buddy in the program is special and important in their own way. Each has a unique personality, and things they like and dislike. Unfortunately, it can be hard to get to knoww someone with IDD. So here is a little "all about me" a few of the Best Buddies.
If you want updates about the Best Buddies students and want to keep updated on events, follow the GHS Best Buddies twitter account.

Caitie

Caitie is a freshman who likes to play on her trampoline. She is very sweet, and so empathetic that when one of her friends' cries, she does too. She likes the color purple and likes to get her nails painted. Like manhy girls her age, she likes to gossip and talk about who is best friends with who.

Ethan

Ethan is a junior who is very energetic. He is always talking to someone, and very playful. He likes to dance and is so popular that he got Homecoming Prince. Being around Ethan, you can't help but be happy because of his gregarious attitude.

Gustavo

Gustavo is a senior who wants to go to Ohio State University. He is kind and funny, and loves to make new friends. He is also popular and this year he won the title of Homecoming King. He loves football games and loves to have fun with his friends.

Jose

Jose is a freshman who always thinks of others. He likes to do whatever Caitie is doing, and always asks her for hugs. He is very sweet, but prefers to be quiet unless he's around Caitie. When he's with her, he lights up.

Nate

Nate is a freshman who is so friendly, at Best Buddies events a crowd of his friends is always with him. He likes to be included in school activities and is full of spirit. He is an honorary Spirit Rustler, and always helps them with their skits. He is always talkative and funny.

Terrence

Terrence is a senior who loves technology. He is shyer than his bestie, Gustavo, but he is so sweet when you get to know him. Always smiling, he makes any place he is a happier place. He loves to help others and is thoughtful.
